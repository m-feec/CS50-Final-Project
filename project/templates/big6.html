01

American Eagle

Produced by the US West Point Mint. It Contains one ounce of .999 fine silver.

The most expensive silver coin from the big 6, with a mark-up premium of around 65% over silver spot price.

Recognizable all around the world with decent quality.

02

Austrian Philharmonic

Produced by The Austrian Mint. It Contains one ounce of .999 fine silver.

Low mark-up premium silver coin from the big 6 with a mark-up premium of around 35% over silver spot price.

Love by investors, the annual average mintage of over 11 ounces.

03


Kruggerand

Produced by The South African Mint. It Contains one ounce of .999 fine silver.

Successor to the oldest private-ownership gold investment coin.

The mark-up premium is around 45%.

04


Britannia

Produced by The Royal Mint. It Contains one ounce of .999 fine silver.

Low mark-up premium silver coin from the big 6 with a mark-up premium of around 35% over silver spot price.

Silver Britannia since 2021 mintage the world's most visually secure bullion coin.

05

Canadian Maple Leaf

Produced by The Royal Canadian Mint. It Contains one ounce of .9999 fine silver.

One of two silver bullion coins made with .9999 fine silver.

MINTSHIELD system is the world's first solution for reducing white spots on every coin dated 2018 or later.

06


Kangaroo

Produced by The Perth Mint. It Contains one ounce of .9999 fine silver.

The second silver bullion coin was made with .9999 fine silver.

Frequently Kangaroo is the best quality to price ratio coin on this list. Mark-up premium is around 35%.
